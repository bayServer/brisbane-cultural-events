Games date window

A simple overview of the two major event periods planned for Brisbane 2032.

Olympic Games

23 July – 8 August 2032

This is the main Olympic competition window and the period most visitors are likely to plan around.

Paralympic Games

24 August – 5 September 2032

This follows the Olympics and extends the tourism and event period across Brisbane and surrounding locations.
